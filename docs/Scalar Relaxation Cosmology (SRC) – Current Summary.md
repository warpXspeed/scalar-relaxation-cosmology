

### Scalar Relaxation Cosmology (SRC) – Current Summary

**Core Foundation**  
SRC proposes one single, invisible **energy crystal substrate** called φ (the aether). This is the fundamental medium from which everything else emerges. We do not claim to fully understand its microscopic nature — it remains largely a black box — but we can observe its effects and traces.

**The Two Coexisting Worlds**  
- **Organized World (Potential Energy State)**: The native, structured, low-entropy state of φ. It has intrinsic self-organizing intelligence, frequency-dependent lattice behavior, non-linear (non-Newtonian-like) response, and acts as a reservoir of latent energy.  
- **Chaotic World (Kinetic Energy State)**: The visible universe we experience — particles, atoms, EM fields, gravity, cosmic structure, and expansion. This is the dynamic, higher-entropy layer of cast-off defects and emergent phenomena that ride on / within the substrate.

**Black Holes as the Third State**  
Black holes represent the **collapse/transition state**, where kinetic energy is compressed to extremes and the chaotic layer begins to merge back into the organized substrate.

Together they form a **natural cycle**:  
**Potential (organized φ) → Kinetic (visible universe) → Collapse (black holes) → Potential**

**What Wigner Crystals Tell Us**  
The Wigner crystal is our clearest current window. It shows that the substrate φ has real structure and a strong self-organizing tendency. Electrons (defects) spontaneously form an ordered triangular lattice when local density and latent energy (temperature) are in the right balance. This is not the aether itself, but a **trace** — like iron filings revealing invisible magnetic field lines. It demonstrates the substrate’s capacity for intelligent, correlated ordering.

**Galactic Currents and Stars**  
Galactic Birkeland currents are real, large-scale flows of plasma and energy enabled by the responsive substrate φ. They act as a major energy transport mechanism. The Sun and other stars are plasma structures that serve as nodes in this galactic circuit. Galactic currents provide significant power and help regulate long-term stability. Nuclear fusion may occur as a secondary side reaction, but it is not the primary driver.

**Planets and Geological Activity**  
Planets also receive supplemental energy from these galactic currents. This external input helps explain excess internal heat, polar shifts, increased seismic and volcanic activity, and other anomalies that cannot be fully accounted for by residual heat or radioactive decay alone.

**Key Principles**  
- Nature is simple and repeats what works. The same self-organizing rules operate across scales, producing different patterns under different conditions (density, latent energy, dominant frequency modes).
- We do not add ad-hoc patches or invisible entities to force the model to fit. We build from observable traces (especially Wigner crystals) and common-sense scaling.
- Much remains unknown. We are reverse-engineering an invisible substrate with limited data and limited lifespan. The Wigner crystal is only a cursory peek; the full picture is still hidden.

**Current Status**  
SRC is a work in progress. It is more minimal and grounded in observable self-organization than standard physics, but it is not complete. We continue to test ideas against real data and the principle of “nature repeats what works” without forcing solutions.
