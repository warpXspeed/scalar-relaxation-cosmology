## Introduction

The direct imaging of **Wigner crystals** (Princeton 2024–2025 and subsequent experiments) provides one of the strongest experimental anchors for SRC’s concept of the scalar substrate field φ as a responsive **energy crystal**.

## What is a Wigner Crystal?

When electrons are cooled to extremely low temperatures and diluted to very low densities in a clean 2D system (e.g., bilayer graphene under strong magnetic fields), they spontaneously arrange into a perfect triangular lattice **without any atomic scaffolding**. The ordering is driven purely by mutual Coulomb repulsion.

This was predicted by Eugene Wigner in 1934. For nearly 90 years it remained theoretical or indirectly inferred. Recent scanning tunneling microscopy has now produced real-space images of this electron lattice.
