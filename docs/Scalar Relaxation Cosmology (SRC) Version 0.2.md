Scalar Relaxation Cosmology (SRC)
Version 0.2 — April 2026
Repository: https://github.com/warpXspeed/scalar-relaxation-cosmologyCore FoundationThe universe consists of two coexisting layers:The Organized Substrate (φ / Energy Crystal / Aether): An invisible, intangible medium with intrinsic structure and self-organizing properties. We cannot see or touch it directly.
The Chaotic Layer: The visible universe we observe — particles, atoms, EM, gravity, cosmic structure, and expansion. This is the emergent, dynamic expression that rides on / within the substrate.

The Wigner crystal experiments provide our clearest current window: electrons (defects) self-organize into a triangular lattice, revealing a trace of the substrate’s underlying structure — similar to iron filings revealing magnetic field lines. The lattice is not the aether itself, but evidence that the aether has organized structure capable of guiding defects into ordered patterns.Key PrinciplesNature is simple and repeats what works. The same basic self-organizing rules operate across scales, producing different patterns under different conditions (density, latent energy/temperature, dominant frequency modes).
The substrate φ exhibits crystal-like, fluid-like, and reservoir-like behavior, with a significant degree of emergent self-organization ("intelligence").
Particles and atoms are cast-off defects/islands from the substrate that attempt to mimic its organizing patterns at their own scale.
Cosmic-scale phenomena (gravity, structure formation, apparent dark energy) are primarily higher-level emergent effects driven by EM and gravity acting within the chaotic layer, while still being constrained by the substrate.

Current Limitations & HonestyWe do not yet fully understand the microscopic nature of φ. The scaling from lab Wigner crystals to cosmic densities remains an open challenge. Black holes may represent a third state or boundary region where the chaotic layer interacts intensely with the organized substrate. Much of our current understanding is inference from limited windows (Wigner crystals being the best one).We are not claiming a complete theory.
