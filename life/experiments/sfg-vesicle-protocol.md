# Experimental Protocol: SFG + Vesicle Coherence

**Objective**: Test frequency-dependent coherence and hysteresis in mineral-water-vesicle systems.

**Materials**
- Decanoic acid + 10% oleic acid
- Pyrite and quartz powder
- Weak thermal or EM cycling

**Protocol**
1. Prepare vesicle-mineral suspensions
2. Adapted sample: 0.15 Hz cycling for 2 hours
3. Control sample: random cycling
4. Measure SFG signal every 5 min after drive removal

**Predictions**
- 20–40% higher SFG intensity in adapted samples
- Slower decay of coherence in pre-exposed samples
- Enhanced vesicle stability and clustering in resonant conditions
