# Budding and Heritable Adaptation

## Rules

- Domains = connected high-M0 + high-coherence clusters
- Growth at edges proportional to local catalyst and coherence
- Budding when size and fitness exceed thresholds
- Daughter inherits averaged spectral state + molecular concentrations with Gaussian noise

## Selection

Fitness ≈ size × CS_mean × M0_mean × M1_mean

Adapted lineages (pre-seeded high coherence + catalyst) show:
- Faster recovery after environmental stress
- Higher budding rates
- Diverging cumulative fitness

This demonstrates **proto-Darwinian evolution** at the compositional-spectral level.
