### 1. Core Ontological Picture of the Substrate (φ)

The substrate is best described as an **energy crystal** — a high-density scalar displacement field that functions as a "strange battery" reservoir of latent strain energy.  

**Common core energy**  
Pure scalar displacement strain energy stored as deviation from equilibrium:  
ρ_φ ≈ ½ (∂_t φ)² + ½ |∇φ|² + V(φ)  
All emergent phenomena (electromagnetism, attraction, spins, quantum wakes, thermal dissipation) are patterned reorganizations of this single undifferentiated strain energy.

**Stable ground state**  
Uniform low-gradient equilibrium (φ ≈ φ_eq, minimal strain). Latent "battery" at rest, self-sustaining, resonant at f_res (cosmic heartbeat from CMB low-ℓ and pulsar data).

**Dynamic properties**  
- Crystal-like: elastic shear (G_shear) for ordered resonance and transverse modes.  
- Newtonian/fluid-like when shocked: viscous damping (γ), dilatant hardening under high strain rates (non-linear γ(φ, φ̇)).

### 2. Repeating Cycle of the Strange Battery

1. **Absorption** — Shocks/perturbations (η fluctuations, gradients) charge the battery by increasing displacement strain.  
2. **Stable formation/growth** — Energy "wants to spin" into topological defects (vortices/Hopfions with conserved winding W). Sympathetic harmonic resonance (f_res coupling) synchronizes spins and amplifies cohesion.  
3. **Overload threshold** — Stress exceeds dilatant hardening capacity.  
4. **Release/breakdown** — Sudden hardening collapse ejects energy as:  
   - EM (piezoelectric shear modes via χ)  
   - Quantum wakes (hysteresis memory trails)  
   - Particles (stable spins/defects)  
   - Thermal/second-sound waves (γ dissipation)  
5. **Devolution & return** — Energies fade back toward uniform equilibrium (irreversible γ arrow). Time is the measurable fading of organized energies.

### 3. Key Conceptual Hooks (for Outreach & Documentation)

1. **There but not there**  
   Omnipresent latent energy reservoir, yet ghostly/invisible until disrupted (neutrino-sea analogy).

2. **Vis neutra**  
   Neutral reformation drive — monopole-like attraction minimizing gradients, no opposites/repulsion.

3. **Relaxation sink pull**  
   Density gradients create sinks; viscoelastic substrate flows inward to minimize and reform uniformity.

4. **Energy crystal substrate**  
   Stable resonant lattice of strain energy; Newtonian when shocked; universe emerges from battery defects.

5. **Sympathetic harmonic spin**  
   Resonance at f_res synchronizes spins and attractions across scales, amplifying stability and growth.

6. **Butterfly hardening collapse** (Primordial Event)  
   Sudden snap from fluid-crystal equilibrium to hardened Newtonian state when strain threshold is exceeded — analogous to northern lake ice cracking under winter stress. Propagating "crack arms" eject EM/quantum radiation/particles, seeding cosmic web filaments as birthplaces of stars.

7. **Scaled devolution**  
   Biology’s breakdown is micro-scale return to simpler matter states, mirroring cosmic fade (not limited by cosmological redshift).

8. **Galactic nova devolution**  
   Central supermassive black hole overload/breakdown releases quasar-scale energy in reformative flares (EM primary). Triggered by accretion overload, dust ingestion, or galactic current sheet connection.

9. **Quasar overload devolution**  
   Observable signature of SMBH form collapse — jets, EM floods, feedback as substrate reforming.

10. **Time as devolution**  
    Measurable irreversible fading of organized energies back into uniform aether equilibrium (primitive in γ term).

### 4. Phenomena & SRC Interpretation

- **Black holes** — Extreme stable knots in the energy crystal; spherical shape maximizes vis neutra compression; devolve slowly via γ evaporation and surface regulation; can trigger galactic novas when overloaded.
- **Stellar novas** — Circuit overload of stellar "plasma engine" (resonant defect cluster in φ), not isolated nuclear collapse. Triggers: dust/charged material ingestion or connection to galactic current sheet (emergent resonant flows in substrate). EM is primary release form.
- **Galactic current sheets** — Large-scale emergent shears/resonant currents in φ (inspired by Alfvén/Birkeland currents), powering stellar engines via EM primacy.
- **Cosmic web filaments** — Propagating "crack arms" from Butterfly snap, channeling energy and seeding star birthplaces.
